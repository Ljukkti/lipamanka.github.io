Here are some toki pona related projects I have either made or taken part in:

  • I created my semantic spaces dictionary, the first dicitonary for toki pona that describes the meaning of all common words in prose instead of having a list of words in a different language (featured on tokipona.org) and many other resources available on this website
  • I helped with many of the definitions on linku.la
  • I contributed to the Toki Pona Dictionary and The Wizard of Oz: Toki Pona Edition, both written by Sonja Lang
  • I got third place in a writing contest (here is my submission)
  • I have designed the logos for kalama sin and the discord servers "ma pali" and "sona.pona.la," and some more of my sitelen suwi art can be seen on the walls of some VR chat worlds as decoration (also featured on Jonathan Gabel's website).
  • I've made two popular fonts: linja sike and linja lipamanka, both known for high levels of interaction with community ideas and submissions (including a couple dozen community submitted jaki and ko glyphs in linja lipamanka) and the popularization of name glyphs
  • Along with Sonja Lang and two other tokiponists (ijo Mina and jan Sa), I developed the official sitelen pona glyph for ku in 2021
  • I've attended toki pona meetups in Washington DC, San Francisco, and Chicago
  • I am a maintainer of linku and nimi ale pona, and I also own "nimi ale," an obscure dictionary of rare nimisin originating in the discord server known as "ma pona pi toki pona" compiled completely by kule epiku Atawan
